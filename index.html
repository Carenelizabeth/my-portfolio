About Me

I'm a full-stack javascript developer. And when I say "full-stack," I really mean is that I can't make up my mind. I love visual design and am giddy if I can seamlessly blend interactive and static elements. When I learned about React components and Redux form validation, I did a little happy dance.

But I also enjoy the challenge of dissecting a third-party API, writing a tricky unit test and implementing RESTful APIs. I geek out over databases and finding the best way to store information.

In my pre-coding life, I freelanced at a photographer and I still love getting out my camera and exporing light and color in the non-virtual world. I relax by making things with my hands and listening to audiobooks.

Once upon a time, my very large family gathered at Lake Tahoe to enjoy a beautiful vacation. We ended up spending most of our time in a hotel room trying to decide what to do next. Family Reunion was born from a desire for a large group of people to easily decide who wants to do what. One participant posts an activity and others can join in or discuss.
