Family Reunion

Once upon a time, my very large family gathered at Lake Tahoe to enjoy a beautiful vacation. We ended up spending most of our time in a hotel room trying to decide what to do next. Family Reunion was born from a desire for a large group of people to easily decide who wants to do what. One participant posts an activity and others can join in or discuss.
